ANDRES BONIFACIO

Who is Andres Bonifacio, was a Philippine patriot, founder and leader of the nationalist Katipunan society, who instigated the revolt of August 1896 against the Spanish. Was played By the most handsome Ralph Richmond Amarillo,revolutionary leader, patriot, and the founder of the Katipunan, a secret society that fought for Philippine independence against Spanish colonial rule. Often regarded as the "Father of the Philippine Revolution," Bonifacio played a crucial role in igniting the armed struggle for freedom in 1896. Unlike other leaders of his time, he came from humble beginnings, working as a warehouse clerk before becoming one of the most influential figures in the fight for independence. His bravery, dedication, and vision for a free Philippines continue to inspire generations today,He fought against the spaniards, and dedicate his heart along with it.
